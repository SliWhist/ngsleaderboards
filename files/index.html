Leaderboard 2.0 has Launched.
Version 2.0 of the Unofficial NGS Leaderboard has been released.
Features include:
  • User accounts.
    You can now sign up and sign in for a handful of extra features! More will come later.
    If you were on the leaderboard already, DM me (SliWhist) on Discord after making an account to begin migrating your data!
  • Translation support.
    Unavailable at launch, but eventually the leaderboard will feature extra translations!
    Thank you to Ondine for asking the question that led to this feature existing!
  • A run submission system.
    As of launch only Solo runs can be submitted, with duos and higher coming later.
    Apologies for the feature regression!
    Notice: An account is required to submit runs.
  • Player name customization.
    Player names can now be set to colors, gradients, or glows.
    This was a feature of Leaderboard 1.0, but went unused!
    Notice: An account is required to customize your name.
  • Runner profiles.
    An expansion of Leaderboard 1.0's runner info panel!
    These panels can feature links for up to two Youtube channels, and more!
    You can also read a runner's description!
    Notice: Custom descriptions and more functionality will come in a future update.
  • Moderation systems.
    A backend feature, but notable in any case.
    This feature currently only enables the approval and denial of runs.
